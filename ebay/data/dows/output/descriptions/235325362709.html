ToolTreaux Complete Gold Silver Platinum Testing Solution Kits with Stone

The ToolTreaux Complete Testing Kit includes bottles of acid testing solution for silver, gold, and platinum along with a testing stone measuring 2.25" x 1.75", with each bottle holding 0.5 ounces. To use, scratch your item onto the stone to leave a visible deposit, then apply a drop of solution and observe the reaction—cream indicates high-quality silver, black indicates coin quality, green indicates high copper content, and gold indicates a brass or plated item, while gold or platinum deposits that dissolve indicate a lower karat or purity.
