Whether for gold prospecting equipment or rock hounding equipment, these all plastic dry sift screens or rock screening pans making the classification of unwanted raw materials a breeze. Simply stack the three sifting screens on top of each other, and then on top of a 3 or 5 gallon bucket; start with the 1/2 inch classifier on the top, followed by the 1/4 inch in the middle, and then the 1/8 inch at the bottom. Once you’ve added your raw material, flush with water; this will push smaller, valuable paydirt down through to the bucket reading for panning or sluicing, while keeping large unwanted organic material, like rocks, gravels, plant matter and aggregates out.

There are also two important design features to note: depth and built in handles. The depth of this plastic sifting screen reaches six inches and will allow you to move through much more raw material in comparison to a shallower classifier screen. And, when carrying and working with heavier and large amounts of material, you'll appreciate the two built in side handles, making the plastic sifting screen easier to maneuver (and easier on the hands too).

ASR Outdoor heavy duty plastic sifting screens are made of tough polypropylene plastic that will see you through many gold prospecting or rock hounding seasons to come. The high quality plastic material will never rust, making it a great classifier suitable for all types of weather. To keep in best condition, rinse and air dry between uses.
