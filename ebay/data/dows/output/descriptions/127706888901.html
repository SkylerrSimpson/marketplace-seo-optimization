GOLD TESTING METHOD

This acid testing method is best used on raw or scrap gold due to the strength of the solution. When testing finished jewelry or delicate pieces, always scratch the item in an inconspicuous area to avoid visible damage.

Instructions:

-Scratch the gold item onto the testing stone, leaving a visible streak. -Apply a small drop of 10K testing solution onto the streak. -If the gold is 10K or higher, the streak will remain unchanged. -If the gold is lower than 10K, the streak will dissolve or fade. -Apply an equal amount of neutralizing solution after each test. -Thoroughly rinse and neutralize the testing stone before additional testing.
