Are you sick of pesky flies and other insects in your house? Then you need a set of PIC Fly Traps.

Our fly traps are an effective way to control common horse flies, houseflies, moths, and other insects both indoors and out. These traps can be used in any room of your house, including food preparation areas as well as outdoors. The modular design increases the surface area of the trap, allowing a greater ability to trap flying insects.

Key Features

  • JUST PEEL AND HANG - Super easy to use, these fly traps will get to work controlling pests right out of the box. Just remove the protective paper and suspend using the attached plastic hook.
  • SAFE FOR INDOOR AND OUTDOOR USE - PIC fly traps can be used outdoors, in any room of your house and even in food preparation areas. They do not contain any vapors and don't make a mess.
  • LONG LASTING - Our fly traps are designed to attract pests for up to 3 months. For maximum results, replace the traps every 3 months.
  • TRUSTED PIC BRAND - You can count on PIC fly traps to get the job done. Our glue attracts and traps flying insects without using harmful sprays or vapors.

The PIC fly traps are super easy to use. Just remove them from the box, carefully remove the protective paper and suspend using the attached plastic hook. Please be careful to not touch the sticky coating by handling the top and the bottom.

For the best results, we suggest that you hang the traps vertically with all sides of the trap exposed, near the center of the area in which you wish to control insects. To discard, wrap the used trap securely in newspaper, paper, or a plastic bag.

PIC fly traps will become even more effective after the first few flies are caught. Since flies are sensitive to temperature, light, and humidity, these traps work great close to windows. Replace the traps every 3 months for the best results.
