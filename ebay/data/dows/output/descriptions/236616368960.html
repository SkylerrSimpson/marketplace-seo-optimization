SILVER TESTING KIT METHOD: This silver testing method is best used on silver that is yet to be smelted, due to the harsh nature of the solution that could potentially damage an already finished item; if testing fine jewelry, sterling silver items or other finished items, be sure to choose an inconspicuous spot so as not to compromise the finished piece.

INSTRUCTIONS: To test silver purity, scratch the item onto the test stone leaving a visible deposit. Place a small drop solution over the top of the deposit; the purity of the silver is approximated according to the color the solution turns when it is applied to the sample. If the solution turns bright red, that indicates high quality silver. If the solution turns blue or green, the sample may contain brass or copper. If the sample deposit dissolves quickly, or the solution turns a dark brown or yellow, it is likely a base metal or silver plated. When done, add an equal amount of neutralizing solution to neutralize the silver solution. Ensure the testing stone has been thoroughly washed and neutralized before performing any testing.

PLEASE NOTE: Testing solutions can be harmful to the skin. Please exercise the utmost caution when opening and using this product and method of testing! Proper protective eye wear and gloves are highly recommended, as the solution can burn unprotected skin. Ensure neutralization of the testing solution after every test by using the neutralizing solution. Read the label on the bottle and follow provided operational instructions.
