The Scoop N Probe has a unique design - it also doubles as a coin probe for locating precious metals with a metal detector. The brass probe easily screws onto the handle for a convenient design allowing for less clutter in the field.

Easy to use, the sand scoop and probe combo tool pairs perfectly with a metal detector.
