The ASR Outdoor 7pc Gold Panning Kit has the essential tools you need for every gold panning and prospecting adventure. You can easily classify your findings with the sifting and dual-riffled pans. The dual riffles catch gold flakes, and the mesh sifting pan allows you to screen off rock and gravel.

Key Features

  • COMPLETE 7PC KIT - Includes two gold pans, a sifting classifier, 2 glass vials, tweezers and a snifter bottle - everything you need to start panning right out of the box.
  • HEAVY-DUTY GOLD PANS - Made from tough ABS plastic and designed with dual riffles (coarse and fine) to maximize gold capture in any environment.
  • PRECISION SNIFTER BOTTLE - Lightweight and easy to use, the 4oz snifter bottle allows for accurate suction and collection of fine gold particles.
  • SECURE GLASS VIALS FOR STORAGE - Two compact vials with secure caps make it easy to safely store and transport your gold finds in the field.
  • IDEAL FOR ALL SKILL LEVELS - Whether you're a beginner or experienced prospector, this compact kit is perfect for streambed gold panning or backyard testing.

You can use the tweezer-magnifier and the snifter bottle to retrieve small size units, then store your valuables in the transparent glass vials for safekeeping. To use the snifter bottle, simply squeeze it to create a vacuum inside, so the submerged gold can easily be sucked up through the tube.
