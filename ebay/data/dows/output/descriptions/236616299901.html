GOLD TESTING METHOD: This gold testing method is best used on gold that is yet to be smelted, due to the harsh nature of the solution that could potentially damage an already finished item. If testing fine jewelry or other finished gold items, be sure to choose an inconspicuous spot, so as not to compromise the finished piece.

INSTRUCTIONS: To test gold karat content, scratch the item onto the test stone leaving a visible deposit. Place a small drop of solution on top of the deposit; if the gold is the same karat or higher, the solution will not affect the deposit. If the deposit is of a lower karat, the deposit will dissolve and eventually disappear. When done, add an equal amount of neutralizing solution to neutralize the gold solution. Ensure gold testing stone has been thoroughly washed and neutralized before starting any more testing!

PLEASE NOTE: Testing solutions can be harmful to the skin. Please exercise the utmost caution when opening and using this product and method of testing! Proper protective eye wear and gloves are highly recommended, as the solution can burn unprotected skin. Ensure neutralization of the testing solution after every test by using the neutralizing solution. Read the label on the bottle and follow provided instructions.
