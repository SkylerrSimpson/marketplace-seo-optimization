TESTING FOR GOLD: To test for karat content or purity, scratch the item on to the test stone leaving a visible deposit, then place a small drop of the solution over top of deposit; if gold is the same karat or higher the solution will not affect the deposit; if the deposit is of a lower karat, the deposit will dissolve

TESTING FOR PLATINUM: For platinum repeat the same process as above; if the material is pure platinum, the deposit will not be affected and the solution will not change

TESTING FOR SILVER: For silver, repeat the same process as above; if the material is of high quality silver, the solution will turn bright red; if the solution turns blue or green the sample may contain brass or copper; if the sample deposit dissolves quickly, or the solution turns a dark brown or yellow, it is likely a base metal
