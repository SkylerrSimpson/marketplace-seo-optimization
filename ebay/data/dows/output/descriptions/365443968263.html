ASR Outdoor 9" 440 Stainless Steel Recurve Full Tang Fixed Blade Hunting Knife

The nine inch Fixed Recurve Blade Full Tang Hunting Knife with wood handle from ASR Outdoor is engineered for precision and versatility in the field. For chopping, slashing, peeling and other base to point cutting tasks, this knife can do it all.

If you’re not familiar with a recurve blade, it means that the knife blade itself is shaped like the letter ‘S’ with a concave edge. The fixed recurve blade lengthens the cutting edge without making the blade longer; it places the edge at multiples to the work being done, allowing it to easily handle different cutting angles.
