The collection bottles are designed for use during gold panning cleanup, concentrate separation or suctioning fine gold from gold pans, dredge or sluice box matting. When gold panning, especially in the final stages of separating gold from black sand, gold particles can be too small to pick out by hand; the collection bottle allows the prospector to easily suction up fine particles, without disturbing surrounding material, and without taking up too much excessive sand or silt in the process.

These fine gold recovery collection bottles are easy to use: After gold is panned down to very fine sediment or concentrates, the prospector visually identifies flakes of gold in their pan or matting. Then, use the suction bottle by placing the tip near the gold and gently squeeze; upon release of the squeeze of the bottle, the suction created pulls the gold into the bottle for safe keeping. Secure fitting nozzle ensures safe storage and transport of collected gold or other precious metals.

These ASR Outdoor fine gold recovery collection bottles are vital for recovering small quantities of gold that may otherwise be lost, especially for amateur and small scale prospectors where every grain counts. Assorted pack sizes available.
