ASR Outdoor Gold Classifier Sifting Prospecting Pan 1/100" Inch Mesh Screen

This extra small particle screen is made of tough, high-impact plastic and 0.1mm stainless steel wire. The design is made to fit easily into a 5 gallon bucket and it matches the size of our 14" gold pans to allow easy transfer of materials from one to the other.
