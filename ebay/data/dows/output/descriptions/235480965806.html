ASR Outdoor Gold Classifier Sifting Prospecting Pan 1/50" Inch Mesh

The ASR Outdoor Gold Classifier is a fine-mesh sifting pan with a 1/50" mesh size (2,500 holes per square inch), built from tough high-impact plastic and 0.1mm 304 stainless steel wire. It's sized to fit a standard 5 gallon bucket and matches the diameter of a 14" gold pan for easy material transfer, in green.

Key Features

  • 304 Stainless Steel Wire: 0.1mm wire thickness for durability
  • Fine Mesh Size: 1/50" mesh, 2,500 holes per square inch
  • Fits a 5 Gallon Bucket: Matches the size of a 14" gold pan for easy transfers
  • Compact and Stackable: Space-saving design for use with other classifiers

Product Specifications

  • Color: Green
  • Country of Origin: China
  • Featured Refinements: Classifier/Sifter
  • Size: 1/50 in Mesh

Classifying your raw material by size before panning helps ensure that gold flakes and nuggets don't get lost among larger rocks, and speeds up your overall recovery process. Compact and stackable, this fine screen is ideal for final clean-up of concentrate before running it through a gravity concentrator bowl.
