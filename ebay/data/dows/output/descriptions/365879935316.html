ASR Outdoor 8" Inch Green Dual Riffle Gold Panning Pan for Mining Prospecting

The ASR Outdoor 8 inch Dual Riffle Gold Pan is molded from one piece of high impact ABS plastic in green. It features both deep and shallow riffles cut into the sidewalls and is UV resistant, rust free, and floats.

Key Features

  • Before First Use: Season the pan by washing off factory production oils

Use the deep riffles to rough out raw material and the shallow riffles to finish off your heavy gold concentrates, taking much of the hard work out of panning. Lightweight and tough, it is built to hold up through years of gold prospecting for newcomers and seasoned prospectors alike.
