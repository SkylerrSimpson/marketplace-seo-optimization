ASR Outdoor Technora Survival Cord Rope 400lb Break Strength

The ASR Outdoor Technora Survival Cord is rated at 400 lb break strength and, weight for weight, is described as 8 times stronger than steel and 3 times stronger than fiberglass, polyester, or nylon yarns. It has a thermal decomposition threshold of 930 degrees Fahrenheit and resists acids, alkalis, and seawater. A PTFE protective coating guards against UV damage and improves flexibility, knot strength, and chemical resistance. It's available in lengths from 25 to 1000 ft, in black.
