The Gear Aid Tenacious Tape Neoprene Patch (previously known as Iron Mend) is a black, 10 by 6 inch iron-on fabric patch for repairing neoprene gear. Cut it to size and activate the adhesive with a household iron to create a permanent repair on neoprene waders, wetsuits, and gloves, and to reinforce seams and high-wear areas like elbows, knees, and underarms. It also repairs stretch fabrics such as spandex and Lycra.
