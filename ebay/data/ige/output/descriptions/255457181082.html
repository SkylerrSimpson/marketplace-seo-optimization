GEAR AID Seam Grip Waterproof Sealant Adhesive for Tents, Outdoors, Clear, 1 oz

GEAR AID Seam Grip WP is a clear, 1 oz waterproof sealant and adhesive for tents, tarps, awnings, and other outdoor gear. This urethane adhesive bonds to nylon, polyester, vinyl, and canvas, seals up to 12 feet of seams, cures into a flexible rubber, and includes two applicator brushes.

Key Features

  • Waterproof Sealant & Adhesive: Repairs holes and seals seams on tents, tarps, and awnings
  • Seals Up to 12 Feet: 1 oz tube; also available in 8 oz
  • Multi-Fabric Bond: Bonds to nylon, polyester, canvas, leather, and vinyl
  • Flexible Cure: Won't peel or crack over time; includes two applicator brushes

Product Specifications

  • Type: standard
  • Features: Waterproof, Adhesive
  • Size: 1oz
  • Suitable For: Camping, Gear Repair, Outdoor

When a seam leaks or a tent tears mid-trip, Seam Grip WP makes a permanent, flexible repair that moves with the fabric instead of cracking or peeling. It's compact enough to pack along for camping, RVing, or boating whenever gear needs a quick fix.
