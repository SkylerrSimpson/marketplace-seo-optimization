GEAR AID Aquaseal FD Flexible Repair Adhesive for Outdoor Gear and Vinyl, Clear

GEAR AID Aquaseal FD is a flexible, waterproof repair adhesive for outdoor gear and vinyl, in clear. It's a rubber glue that cures in 8-12 hours and bonds to neoprene, nylon, canvas, leather, rubber, and GORE-TEX fabric, and is available in tube sizes ranging from 0.75 oz to 8 oz.

Key Features

  • Waterproof Flexible Adhesive: Clear rubber glue cures in 8-12 hours
  • Multi-Fabric Bond: Sticks to neoprene, nylon, canvas, leather, rubber, and GORE-TEX fabric
  • Durable Repair: Creates a water-tight seal that won't peel or crack over time
  • Abrasion Resistant: Withstands scrapes, scratches, and extreme temperatures
  • Multiple Sizes Available: Tubes ranging from 0.75 oz to 8 oz

Whether it's ripped waders, a torn wetsuit, a leaky inflatable, or worn muck boots, Aquaseal FD gets you back outside fast with a permanent, water-tight seal that resists abrasion and won't peel or crack over time. It's the go-to fix for anglers, paddlers, and anyone whose gear takes a beating in the field.
