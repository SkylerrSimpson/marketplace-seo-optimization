GEAR AID Revivex Durable Water Repellent (DWR) Spray for Reproofing Jackets

Restore the protective DWR (durable water repellency) coating on high-tech rainwear or snow gear with Revivex Durable Water Repellent. This spray-on treatment repels water, oil and stains. It can also be used for tent reproofing. Over time the DWR of outerwear and camp gear wears off. Regular reproofing treatment of jackets, tents, and other gear is highly recommended. The durable formula creates a water repellent coating that stays on even after a wash.
