Gear Aid Paracord and Carabiner 5.5 mm Heavy-Duty Cord for Camping - 100 Feet

Designed to be durable and strong, the 1100 Paracord is for heavy-duty jobs at camp or in the field. The 5.5 mm, 16-strand construction makes it double the strength of our 550 Paracord. It’s tough cordage that offers less stretch – ideal for securing shelter and gear. Use it to tie down tents, tarps, luggage, or equipment on vehicles, kayaks, and rafts. Its burly thickness allows for easier handling when used as a bear hang, clothesline, lightweight anchor (20 lbs or less), and kayak towline. Whether camping, hunting, or paddling, this reflective paracord works hard all day and all night.

Key Features

  • HEAVY DUTY PARACORD - Count on this 5.5 mm thick cord for extra heavy-duty jobs like securing shelter and gear; features twice the strength of our 550 Paracord
  • DURABLE CONSTRUCTION - Made with 100% black nylon, its 16 inner strands can be unraveled for use as a fishing line, thread, and floss
  • REFLECTIVE DESIGN - Use this reflective paracord day or night while camping, hunting, or paddling to tie down tents/tarps, create a bear hang or clothesline, and more
  • CARABINER INCLUDED - Includes a large, aluminum carabiner with twist lock for secure and convenient attachment options
  • NOT FOR CLIMBING - Neither the cord nor the carabiner is designed for climbing. Please do NOT use this cord or the carabiner for climbing.
