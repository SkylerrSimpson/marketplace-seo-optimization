SE Prospector's Choice 12in Pocket Sized Gold Sluice Box, Green

  • Three Riffle Patterns: Hungarian, V-shape, and square riffles in one channel

  • Lightweight TPR Plastic: Corrosion and rust resistant
  • Pocket-Sized & Portable: Fits in a backpack or bag for scouting trips
  • Built For River Panning: Ideal for gold panners and prospectors

Scout a new spot before hauling out your full-size gear—this compact sluice slips into a backpack or bag so you can test the water on the go.
