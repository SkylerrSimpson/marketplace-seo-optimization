Mildew smell, body odor, and other unpleasant scents can ruin expensive gear. Use Revivex Odor Eliminator to get rid of bad smells, not just mask it. Previously known as Mirazyme, this concentrated blend of water-activated microbes removes the bacteria causing the odor. Apply as spray or soak to treat hard to wash items like tents and wetsuits. Remove bad odors from all types of outdoor fabrics naturally with Revivex Odor Eliminator.
