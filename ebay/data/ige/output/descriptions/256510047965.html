GEAR AID Revivex Odor Eliminator for Tents, Footwear and Sports Gear, 2 fl oz

GEAR AID Revivex Odor Eliminator is a concentrated, 2 fl oz formula (formerly Mirazyme) of water-activated microbes that removes odor-causing bacteria from tents, footwear, wetsuits, and sports gear made of neoprene, fleece, spandex, Lycra, or cotton.

Key Features

  • Eliminates Odor-Causing Bacteria: Targets mildew, mold, pet, and perspiration odors
  • Concentrated Formula: Half an ounce treats multiple garments in 20 gallons of water
  • Versatile Application: Spray, soak, or add to a washing machine load
  • Safe On Many Fabrics: Neoprene, nylon, polyester, and GORE-TEX
  • Use Within 72 Hours: Once mixed with water
