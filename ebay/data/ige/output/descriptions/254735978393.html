GEAR AID Aquaseal SR Shoe and Boot Repair Adhesive, Clear Glue, 1 oz

GEAR AID Aquaseal SR is a clear, 1 oz shoe and boot repair adhesive, formerly known as Freesole. It's a urethane glue that dries as a strong, flexible rubber, bonding permanently to leather, suede, rubber, neoprene, canvas, and GORE-TEX boots and shoes, and is unaffected by heat or cold.

Key Features

  • Permanent Footwear Repair: Bonds soles and heels for lasting fixes
  • Clear Flexible Rubber: Dries clear and flexible, won't peel or crack over time
  • Multi-Material Bond: Works on leather, suede, rubber, neoprene, canvas, and GORE-TEX
  • Weather Resistant: Unaffected by heat or cold; abrasion resistant and waterproof
  • 1 oz Tube: Also available in multipacks

  • Color: Clear
  • Size: 1oz

Don't toss worn-out footwear, fix it permanently. Aquaseal SR rebuilds worn heels, creates toe protectors, and stops soles from delaminating, so hiking boots, running shoes, and cleats hold up through years of hard use at the jobsite, on the trail, or on the field.
