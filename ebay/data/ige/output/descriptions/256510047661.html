GEAR AID Care Kit with Pro Cleaner, Wash-In Water Repellent and Mini Patches

The Revivex Wash and DWR Care Kit for GORE-TEX fabrics includes everything you need to keep high-tech gear looking good. Select the appropriate kit, all of which include Revivex Pro Cleaner, a tech wash that effectively removes dirt, grime and stains without stripping off gear’s durable water repellent (DWR) coating. Afterwards, add or restore water repellency on jackets, waders, gloves, and other waterproof breathable fabrics using Revivex Wash-In or Durable. Choose a kit with Tenacious Tape Mini Patches so when holes and rips make an appearance, just peel-and-stick for a repair that lasts. Keep gear in tip-top shape with the Revivex Wash and DWR Care Kit.

Key Features

  • Clean, restore breathability, and protect GORE-TEX fabric/outerwear from water, dirt and stains to keep it looking and performing like new with this easy to use care system
  • Pick the care kit that fits your needs; all kits include 10 fl oz of Revivex Pro Cleaner and 10 fl oz of Wash-In Water Repellent or 10. 5 oz of Durable Water Repellent (DWR) spray, with Tenacious Tape Mini Patches optional
  • Use the concentrated Pro Cleaner to wash up to 20 jackets or 10 sleeping bags; restores breathability and removes dirt and grime from GORE-TEX fabric, eVent, NeoShell, Omni-Tech, and more waterproof breathable materials
  • Restore waterproofing with our Wash-In Water Repellent, a concentrated, fluorocarbon-free formula that treats up to 10 jackets, or use our breathable, washable DWR, a high-performance spray for insulated and non-insulated outerwear
  • Fix tears instantly on GORE-TEX fabric, nylon, polyester and vinyl with peel-and-stick Tenacious Tape Mini Patches; kit includes 3 black nylon and 3 clear 1. 5” x 2. 5” patches
