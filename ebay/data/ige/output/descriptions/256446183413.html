GEAR AID Seam Grip Silicone Sealant for Silnylon Tents and Tarps, Clear 1.5 oz

GEAR AID Seam Grip SIL (formerly SIL Net) is a clear, 1.5 oz silicone sealant formulated specifically for sealing seams on silicone-treated tents, tarps, and backpacks. It cures to a flexible, elastic rubber that bonds only to silicone-treated gear, and one tube can seal most two-person tents.

Key Features

  • Silicone-Specific Formula: Bonds only to silicone-treated (silnylon) gear
  • Seals Up to 24 Feet: 1.5 oz tube covers most two-person tents
  • Flexible, Elastic Cure: Stretches with fabric, won't peel or crack
  • Weather Resistant: Unaffected by extreme heat or cold

Ordinary sealants won't stick to silnylon, so this silicone-specific formula is a must for ultralight tent and tarp owners. It flexes and stretches with the fabric for a long-lasting, waterproof seal that holds up through extreme heat or cold.
