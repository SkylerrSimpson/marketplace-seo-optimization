GEAR AID Silicone Grease, Rubber Conditioner and Lubricant for Dive Gear 0.25 oz

Silicone Grease by GEAR AID lubricates dive gear by soaking deeply into rubber surfaces. It also conditions everything from O-rings to pressure systems and valve systems; protecting expensive equipment from cracking and drying. This multipurpose lubricant helps seal out water and can be used on underwater cameras, waterproof dive lights, pressure systems and more. Reduce damage caused by the elements and keep gear performing at its best, use Silicone Grease regularly before or after a watersports trip.

Key Features

  • Lubricate rubber, plastic and metal parts on dive gear; maintains O-rings that seal out water; available in a 0.25 oz tube
  • Permeate the surface of materials for thorough lubrication of valves, B.C.D.s, and other components
  • Condition and fight against cracking and drying; made of natural, 100% pure food grade silicone
  • Use it to resist oxidation and improve water repellency for breathing apparatus, hoses, and pipe fittings
