GEAR AID Tenacious Tape Iron-On Neoprene Wetsuit Repair Patch, 6"x 10", Black

GEAR AID Tenacious Tape Neoprene Patch (formerly Iron Mend) is a black, 10" x 6" iron-on fabric patch that can be cut to size for permanent repairs on neoprene waders, wetsuits, and other stretch fabrics like spandex and Lycra. Simply cut it to fit and activate the adhesive with a household iron.

Key Features

  • Iron-On Application: Activated with a household iron in minutes
  • 10" x 6" Black Patch: Cut to size for a custom fit
  • For Neoprene & Stretch Fabrics: Also repairs spandex and Lycra
  • Reinforces High-Wear Areas: Elbows, knees, underarms; abrasion resistant

Reinforce high-wear areas like elbows, knees, and underarms before they rip, or fix a tear the moment it happens. This heat-activated patch flexes with the fabric and resists abrasion, extending the life of wetsuits, drysuits, booties, and gloves.
